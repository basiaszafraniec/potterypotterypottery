    Hi. Hello.

    I am Zofia Bekisz


    Welcome to my pottery portfolio! Feel free to browse through my artwork, and don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to connect :)

    How it started...

    I wasn’t always super enthusiastic about art in general. However, after my time in Denmark, I discovered a great passion for creating pottery and ceramics in my free time. It was after my first pottery class in Højskole, where I learned the basics and really grew in my craft. I realized I had a true knack for creating cute dishes and bringing smiles to those who I gift them to.

    I love making others happy with my creations and I’m excited to to share my artwork as inspiration with the online pottery community whom I’ve taken great inspo from as well.

    How it's going...

    For me, pottery is a meditative and therapeutic process. The hands on quality of working with clay really helps me feel connected to my surroundings and allows me to express myself creatively.

    Transforming a blob of clay into something beautiful and functional is definitely my favourite part. Every piece I create tells a story, whether it's about the journey of becoming what it is or the inspiration behind its creation. The whole process is what I fell in love with and I’m excited to share my passion with others.
